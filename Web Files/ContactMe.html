About Us It's Nice to Meet You!

We are a group of seniors at the University of Washington determined to make Seattle Public School data more accessible to all. While the data we have here is publicly available to everyone, our goal is give you all a simple way to explore and compare data across all Seattle schools. We hope this tool delivers that goal and provides a fun and easy tool to view school data.
